FAQ

The details before your first visit.

Do I need a consultation before booking color or extensions?

Yes. Consultations help us assess hair health, timing, pricing, and the best path to your goal.

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend two to four weeks for color and six to eight weeks for bridal or extension services.

Do you work with damaged or previously colored hair?

Absolutely. We create gradual plans that prioritize strength, shine, and realistic color progression.

What products do you recommend after appointments?

Your stylist will prescribe a simple aftercare ritual based on your hair type, color, and styling habits.

Is parking available?

Metered street parking and nearby garage parking are available within a two-minute walk of the studio.
